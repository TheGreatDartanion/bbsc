Virginia Localities and the COVID-19 Response

Virginia localities are responding to COVID-19 for citizens, businesses, and employees.

The Berkley Group and BIRCHbark Strategic Consulting have teamed up to bring you a resource to better understand what policy measures are being taken at the local level.

When working for local government, common question our local managers would ask when thinking about something new was “what are other localities doing?”

This page provides available local government data in one spot. Local government actions are evolving and we will update frequently.
